I helped start a club at my high school that builds catapults. In my senior year of high school, a few of my friends and I were notified by our physics teacher that Front Range Community College was holding a catapult building contest for highschool students. So, this group of friends started spending our lunches in the physics room working on a catapult. Unfortunantly, we found out about the contest a little late to have a completed catapult by the date, but we did end up making a functional catapult. Although our catapult wasn't very impressive, the next year, the catapult club officially started with the next group of seniors.
